Manju Warrier

Actress, producer, classical dancer and playback singer

Manju Warrier is an Indian actress, producer, classical dancer and playback singer who primarily appears in Malayalam language films along with few Tamil Films. She is the recipient of several awards including a National Film Award, Kerala State Award and seven Filmfare Awards.

Early films (1995 – 1999)

Warrier first appeared in a television serial Moharavam which was telecast on Doordarshan.In the first half of her on-screen career, Manju featured in 20 films over a three-year period in Malayalam. At the age of 17, she debuted in the film Sakshyam (1995).[16] Later at the age of 18, she acted in the movie Sallapam (1996).

2015 – present
